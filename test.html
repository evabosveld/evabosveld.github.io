Erich Wichman

Erich Wichman - Vrije Radicaal (17-03-2018 / 24-06-2018)

Exhibition design


A retrospective exhibition about the life of Erich Wichman, an idiosyncratic and influential Utrecht born artist working at the beginning of the twentieth century. He was one of the first abstract artists of his time, and -during his brief life- made an important contribution as a painter, printmaker, poet, chemist and activist. Wichman was a striking and provocative character in the cultural- and political scene of the Netherlands. This documentary exhibition unfolds like a three-dimensional book: there is much to see, read and discover. In addition to exploring Wichman’s art, it also sheds light on his activities as a polemist and political activist.

The design of this exhibition is part of the series of exhibitions of Utrecht based artist that are displayed in de New Utrecht Galleries.

Curated by: Bart Rutten en Lara Stolwerk, Centraal Museum
Research and advice: Frans van Burkom, Joep Haffmans en Marja Bosma

Anonymous Identities

Anonymous Identities

Graduation project #2, product line and identity


Many people use social media to show an interesting and positive image of themselves. Users of other anonymous social websites however, show something completely different: an extremely negative view of humanity and themselves. Both sides are unrealistic, and both show just one side of the story. When are we most open and honest? When we're personally responsible for our words, or when we’re anonymous? Can we get closer to a realistic image if these two extremes balance each other out?

Dear Internet

Dear Internet

Graduation project #1, three short films, 08:10


Our relationship with digital information is complex. Awareness about online privacy is important, but seems to have become a synonym for fear and paranoia. At the same time, products and services with an Internet connection are everywhere, and the amount of social media users keeps rising. I've used myself as an example for others that are in the same state as I am, one of constant confusion about my personal information online.

Koop Een Nieuwe

Koop Een Nieuwe (Buy A New One)

Art (with Nick Beens) and graphic design (with Nick Beens and Vincent Kierkels)


A music video about a perfect world, where constantly spending money is the norm, and sustainability is for losers. Our world isn’t that much different. We’re bombarded with messages that encourage us to fill our lives with disposable products. If something breaks, we immediately throw it out and replace it. This video isn’t made to criticize the individual, but to comment on the lazy mindset of consumerism.

Stalker Style

Stalker Style

A4, multiple types of paper


What does someone really see when they've hacked your webcam? Does that even happen in real life? This book holds a collection of articles, little experiments, tutorials and screenshots. Together they tell multiple sides of this story about watching and being watched. The side of the webcam-hackers, and the side of the hacked. The side of the carefree internetters, and the side of the paranoid ones.

about

I'm Eva. I'm a graphic designer and I live in Utrecht with my cat Koos.

I have a lot of questions and very little answers. But that's ok, I think it's up to people to draw their own conclusions.

To start that train of thought I combine different forms of information, opinions and ideas. Luckily the Internet and its users provide me with me with an endless supply of this. Their complex online cultures and structures fascinate me, and are recurring subjects in my work.

It's very important to me to show things the way they are. I try to tell an honest and multidimensional story, and often experiment with digital media and moving images.
